Privacy Policy

Credit Card Offers (SundAi)

Last Updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Credit Card Offers (SundAi) ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension and web application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Credit Card Offers Data

When you use our Chrome extension to extract offers from credit card websites (American Express, Chase, Capital One), we collect:

  • Merchant names
  • Offer descriptions and discount details
  • Offer expiration dates
  • Offer categories and terms
  • Card issuer information (Amex, Chase, etc.)

1.2 Authentication Information

When you sign in with Google, we collect:

  • Google account email address
  • Google account name
  • Google account profile picture
  • Google account ID (unique identifier)

Note: We use Google OAuth for authentication. We do not store your Google password or have access to your Google account beyond the information you consent to share during sign-in.

1.3 Technical Data

We may collect technical information such as:

  • Browser type and version
  • Extension version
  • Usage statistics (offer extraction counts, sync frequency)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To store and sync your extracted credit card offers across devices
  • Account Management: To identify your account and provide personalized access to your offers
  • Functionality: To enable search, filtering, and export features for your offers
  • Improvement: To improve our extension and web application functionality

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using the following services:

3.1 Supabase

Your extracted offers and account information are stored in Supabase, a secure cloud database service. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

3.2 Local Storage

The Chrome extension may store offer data locally in your browser's storage for offline access and improved performance. This data remains on your device.

3.3 Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication via Google OAuth
  • Row-level security policies in our database
  • Access controls to ensure only you can access your own offers

4. Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we use the following third-party services:

4.1 Google OAuth

We use Google OAuth for user authentication. Google's privacy policy applies to the information they collect during the authentication process: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 Supabase

We use Supabase to store and manage your data. Supabase's privacy policy applies: https://supabase.com/privacy

4.3 Vercel

Our web application is hosted on Vercel. Vercel's privacy policy applies: https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy

5. Browser Extension Permissions

Our Chrome extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To read offer information from credit card websites when you click the extension icon
  • storage: To save extracted offers locally in your browser
  • identity: To enable Google Sign-In functionality
  • Host permissions (americanexpress.com, chase.com): To access and extract offers from these credit card websites
  • tabs: To detect which credit card website you're viewing to apply the correct extraction logic

We only access your data when you actively click the extension icon and choose to extract offers. We do not automatically collect data without your explicit action.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view all your stored offers through the web dashboard
  • Delete: You can delete individual offers or your entire account at any time
  • Export: You can export your offers to CSV format
  • Sign Out: You can sign out at any time, which will clear your local session
  • Account Deletion: To permanently delete your account and all associated data, please visit our website for instructions

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal compliance.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please visit our website to report this issue.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Your continued use of our service after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please visit our website:

Website: https://cc-offers.vercel.app

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and applies to all users of Credit Card Offers (SundAi).
